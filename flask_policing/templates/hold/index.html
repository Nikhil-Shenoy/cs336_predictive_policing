Know when crime strikes

Use San Francisco's Predictive Policing to find and report crime alerts in the San Francisco Bay Area. Reports are submitted by the people for the people to know when crimes happen.

View information on the latest criminal activity in the Bay Area. Find out if your neighborhood is at risk!

Community leaders can use our platform to pinpoint specific issues in their neighborhoods and start a dialogue with police on how to fix those issues.

If you are a law enforcement member, help improve our prediction system by contributing new data as it comes in.

Getting Started

Predictive Policing for the San Francisco Bay Area ensures lower crime rates as individuals are more aware of the daily crime occurrences. As individuals report crimes directly through our Predictive Policing program, we can monitor crimes that take place all throughout the San Francisco Bay Area. To report a crime, please go to Report a Crime. If you have any questions pertaining to Predictive Policing, please feel free to contact us as well!
