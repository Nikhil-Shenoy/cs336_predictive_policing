Explore Our Records

Curious about crime? Take a look at our data covering a complete landscape of crime in San Francisco. Through various searches, analysis of normally overwhelming and tedious data is easy, organized, and accessible. Learn about interesting trends that exist amongst crime offenders and law enforcement in this community, such as possible correlations between law enforcement response and management of arrests and offender demographics. Or, discover the opposite - find interesting statistics about law enforcement and the police officers that protect your community. Whatever you want to know, we can help.



Numerical Search

Numerical Search relies on a simple analysis scheme; you specify two attributes you want to compare, and we return histograms of the first parameter versus the second. We generate a histogram for every value of your second parameter, which provides you with the entire joint distribution of those two parameters

Top Records

If you like to keep things simple, perhaps the Top Records feature is for you. Based on a given paramter, you'll get a table with top N values, where you can specify N.
